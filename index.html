About

The Business League is an organisation designed to develop a network of enriched innovators.

The league makes use of the IFA business system, offered through Clientele Life to provide willing entrepreneurs the opportunity to build a growing monthly income.

The League will achieve its goals by each member following the strategy that is set out below.

Strategy

Become a member of the Business League by applying for an IFA Business Account with Clientele Life.

On successful application, each member becomes a stakeholder of the organisation and is assigned a unique number(aka IFA number).

Stakeholder Responsibility

The responsibility of each stakeholder is to spread the word and grow membership by refering them to this website with your unique IFA number. Why?

Our support consultant will contact each new member and organise a face to face meeting to complete your application for your IFA Business Account.

By fulfilling your responsibility and getting ten members, your IFA business account will will activate an on-going monthly salery directly into your bank account.

At the same time you will be growing the membership organically and this will allow for a wider and more resourceful network of professionals.
