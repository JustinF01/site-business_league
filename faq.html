What is an IFA Business Account and how does it benefit me?

 

The IFA network marketing system has been changing the lives of ordinary South Africans for 16 years. It was created by Clientele Life in 1998 and has paid out R1.6 Billion rand in earnings to all those involved. This account allows you to create a monthly income for yourself by making use of network marketing.

What do I get as a Stakeholder?

 

As a stakeholder you can use this platform to find support and motivation for all your business ideas.
