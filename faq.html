FAQ's

 

Why do I apply for an insurance product when I apply?

 

When opening your IFA Business account, you will have a choice of quality and affordable financial products from Clientele Life. Choosing a product makes it possible to generate an income as earnings are calculated as a percentage of the premium paid for the product received.

 

What is an IFA Business Account and how does it benefit me?

 

The IFA network marketing system has been changing the lives of ordinary South Africans for 16 years. It was created by Clientele Life in 1998 and has paid out R1.6 Billion rand in earnings to all those involved. This account allows you to create a monthly income for yourself by making use of network marketing.

 

By joining this system, you will have the opportunity to make use of the structure to create an additional income for yourself. There are three ways that this account pays you out:

 
  1. Bounuses
  2. Monthly Earnings
  3. Club Points
 

You can find out more by visiting the IFA website.
