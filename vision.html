Vision

To support the development of professionals in order for them to make a positive change in their lives, the lives of others and South Africa as a whole.

Mission

To help and support the professional development of each member by providing quality service.

Goals

  • Empower each member by providing a means to generate a stable monthly income as well as resources to promote entrepreneurship.
  • To grow membership in order to provide effective networking opportunities for all members involved.

Refer to the Strategy to see how the League plans on achieving its goals through your involvement.
